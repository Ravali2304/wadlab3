Screeners

Screeners on Chartink are powerful tools designed to help traders and investors filter stocks based on specific criteria and strategies. They allow users to identify stocks that meet certain technical, fundamental, or custom conditions, making the investment decision-making process more efficient and informed.
Below are the key aspects of Chartink's screeners:

Chartink Screeners Features

  • Customizable Screening: Create filters using technical, fundamental, or custom criteria.
  • Technical Indicators: Includes RSI, MACD, Moving Averages, etc.
  • Fundamental Metrics: Screen stocks by PE ratio, EPS, or market cap.
  • Predefined Screeners: Access ready-made filters like Breakout or High Volume Gainers.
  • Real-Time Data: Screen stocks with live market updates for intraday trading.
  • Alerts & Notifications: Get email or app alerts for screener results.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Build screeners easily with drag-and-drop tools.
  • Multiple Trading Styles: Suitable for intraday, swing, and positional strategies.
