WELCOME TO CHARTINK HOME PAGE

Chartink is a powerful platform for stock market analysis, specifically designed for traders and investors in the Indian stock market. Its main appeal lies in its ability to offer real-time and customizable tools for screening stocks, analyzing trends, and making data-driven decisions.


Why Should Someone Use Chartink?

  • Customizability: Ideal for traders who need specific, tailored stock screening based on their strategies.
  • Time-Efficient: Quickly identifies stocks that meet specific criteria, saving time spent manually analyzing data.
  • Wide Range of Indicators: Caters to both technical and fundamental analysts with its diverse range of parameters.
  • Affordability: Provides many free tools, making it accessible for beginners and small-scale traders.
  • Indian Market Focus: Tailored specifically for the Indian stock market, making it a standout choice for Indian traders and investors.
  • Real-Time Updates: Ensures traders have access to the latest data for informed decision-making.
  • Community Insights: Learn from other traders by exploring their screeners and strategies.
